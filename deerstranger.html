Deer Stranger,

An Intuitive VR Experience for Naive Users

Your car crashes outside snowy Virginia, and the only place you can go to for help is the mysterious cabin in the woods. The radio warnings of the ongoing snow storms and the nearby "bear attacks" seem... not quite right as you rift through the dead stranger's conspiracy board and cryptid-hunting trophies.


ROLE Producer, VR Programmer
TIMELINE 2 weeks

Game Mechanic

Escape room! Major focus on making interactions intuitive to naïve guests, giving them a high degree of agency (while also limiting their range of actions!), and ensuring they perform exactly what we predict they would.

Platform

Created on Unity for Oculus Quest 3.

This project has a focus on introducing new entertainment technology to guests who may not be familar with it, and required us to build a VR world where a naïve user feels they have a high degree of agency, while also limiting their range of actions, so that they would perform the tasks we have predicted they would.

We worked on a horror-themed escape room for this world.


Worked on production and VR programming for all the world's interactions. Also implemented inverse kinematics animations to make the monster always face the VR camera, and turn its head according to the user's position.
