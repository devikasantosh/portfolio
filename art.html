Music App UI

Created in 2 hours for Gradient, a UI ideathon by our universityâ€™s Android Club, one rainy July.
Teammate: Yashvardhan Gupta

We drew inspiration from a vinyl record and tried to implement that classic aesthetic in a modern context. This led us to design a clean and minimalist interface, with a nostalgic touch of circular elements and a vinyl-inspired player.
The color palette was specifically chosen to give a theatrical feel inspired by nightclubs and concerts, and the typography was kept simple to maintain the clean, minimalist aesthetic. We also ensured that the user experience was smooth and intuitive, with easily accessible features and a clear, easy-to-understand layout.
