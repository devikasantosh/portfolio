Treasure Falls

A 2-player experience using DDR pads as controllers!

Our task was to build a multiplayer experience while exploring weird platforms in a week.

We decided to create a 2-player experience with DDR pads. Our world was a 2.5D collaborative game where the players had to communicate with eachother to move together and collect treasures, avoid curses, and strategize to avoid dropping a treasure chest held between them.

  • Time: 1 week.
  • Role: Programmer
  • Software and Platforms: Unity Engine, DDR Pad

Team

Brian He, Fay Li, Enn Ruan, Candice Zhang

Challenges

Creating a multiplayer experience with "weird" and new platforms we had never used for game development before.
